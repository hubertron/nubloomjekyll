I will admit it. As a teenager I listened to happycore, and to be honest I enjoyed it. I danced my ass off till 9am on many occasions. For those of you who may not have heard of this genre here is a great definition curtsey of Wikipedia.org Happycore can be defined as:

 

Happycore is a form of dance music typified by a very fast tempo (usually around 165-180 BPM), often coupled with male or female vocals, and saccharine lyrics. Its characteristically 4/4 beat "happy" sound distinguishes it from most other forms of breakbeat hardcore, which tend to be "darker". In its original incarnation, it was often characterized by piano riffs, synth stabs and spacey effects. This genre of music is closely related to the typically Dutch genre of Gabber.
