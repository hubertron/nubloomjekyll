Le Rug Rekkid release party

With the So So Glos, Frankpollis, Heartwarmer, and Hysterics. The space is pretty raw, and we're supposed to have artwork from SelfPortrait.net. Death By Audio, 49 S 2nd @ Wythe on the L train near the Bedford stop 8p; $5, All ages

 

I Heard Danger 3rd ward insanity and mischief

 

Put your Hands up for NY w/ Green Velvet, the Cut NYC Boys and the Trouble and Bass Crew @ Studio B ... This is one of those parties that you really regret that you miss.

 

Alter-Ego

With rare dancefloor DJ set from import/export on the heavy bass/dub/ dancehall tip, NYC laptop battle champ Velapene Screen playing a live house/techno PA, and DJ Rob Paterson. Plus special guests tasty Brooklyn techno secret YSLE 68 Greenpoint Ave, right next to Coco66 with the blinking bar sign, Brooklyn, G train to Greenpoint station, 10p; $free

 

Older Than Jesus

Live electronics and the facts on wax by D.Vamp Porkchopulous, and from Chicago below underground, with Lins.Ms.Lins aka Lady Juke Box, Zebra Blood, Dirty Finger, and Jeremytropic. Silent Barn 915 Wyckoff, Brooklyn L train to Halsey station 8p doors; $4, free before 10p, or free all night if you're dressed like the messiah
