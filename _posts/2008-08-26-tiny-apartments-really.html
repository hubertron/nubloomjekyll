Sometimes, Less is just Less: the 250 SF Condo for $279K: " Housing in San Francisco is expensive, and one way to make units cost less is to make them smaller, particularly if there is restrictive zoning and you can get more units in the same building envelope. Now they have squeezed units down to 250 square feet, smaller than most hotel rooms. George Hauser, the architect, notes that they aren't for everyone- 'It's not the last place a person might own, but a great place to spend three to five years as a young single ... to build equity and move up,' said Hauser, principal of Hauser Architects in San Francisco. 'You're in a small space with great amenities and the resources of the city.' ...
