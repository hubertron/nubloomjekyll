RuPaul - Supermodel
RuPaul defined an interesting time in music. The club scene was at it 2nd big revival. E was big. Gay men were coming out in a big way in clubs all over NYC clubland and this track was an anthem of sorts to the way people lived during that time.

Scatman John - Scatman
Let's be honest here, this was always a weird song. Scat was a music genre where a person sings very fast. This was pretty much the only song in the genre that went mainstream. Everyone I knew in the 90's knew it and sung it. Listening to it a decade later I have to say its still catchy.
